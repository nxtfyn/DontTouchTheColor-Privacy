🔒 PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: June 14, 2026

📋 OVERVIEW

This Privacy Policy describes how Nextfyn ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you play Don't Touch The Color (the "Game") on Android.

By playing the Game, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the Game.

📊 INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect only what is necessary to provide and improve the Game.

  • Guest ID: A randomly generated anonymous identifier stored locally on your device to track your progress.
  • Google Account (optional): If you sign in with Google, we receive your display name and email address to show your name on the leaderboard.
  • Game scores & stats: Your best score, combo records, level reached, and coins earned — stored locally and optionally on our leaderboard.
  • Device settings: Sound, music, and haptic preferences — stored locally only.
We do not collect your precise location, contacts, phone number, or any other sensitive personal information.

🎯 HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

  • To display your name and score on the global leaderboard
  • To save and restore your game progress across sessions
  • To calculate your rank among other players
  • To improve gameplay experience and fix bugs

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

🔗 THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

The Game uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase (Firestore) — stores leaderboard scores.
    firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Google Sign-In — optional account authentication.
    policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google AdMob — displays advertisements.
    policies.google.com/technologies/ads
AdMob may collect device identifiers and show personalised or non-personalised ads based on your settings. You can opt out of personalised ads in your Android device settings under Google → Ads.

💾 DATA STORAGE & RETENTION

Your game progress (scores, coins, settings) is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. Leaderboard data is stored on Firebase Firestore servers managed by Google.

If you uninstall the Game, locally stored data is deleted automatically. Leaderboard data on Firebase may be retained unless you request deletion.

👶 CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

The Game is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The optional Google Sign-In feature requires users to have a valid Google account.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

⚖️ YOUR RIGHTS

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your leaderboard data
  • Opt out of Google Sign-In and play as a Guest at any time
  • Opt out of personalised ads via Android device settings

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below.

🛡️ SECURITY

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. Leaderboard data is stored on Google Firebase which employs industry-standard security practices. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

🔄 CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the Game after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
